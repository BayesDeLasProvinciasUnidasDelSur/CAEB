Intersubjective agreements in contexts of uncertainty
Promoting Bayesian communities in Latin America, the Caribbean, and the peoples of the Global South. Despite the fact that knowledge under uncertainty is "plural and conditional," colonial-modern science and politics have presented themselves as "singular and definitive". The scientific, cultural, and ecological transition we need in favor of plurality is going to happen.

Towards the Plurinational Bayesian Congress!

August 4 and 5, 2023 - Santiago del Estero, Argentina
