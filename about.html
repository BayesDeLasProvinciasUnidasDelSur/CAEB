The Plurinational Bayesian Congress (CBP) aims to bring together students, teachers, researchers, practitioners and experts who use, develop or implement Bayesian methods both in academia and in industry.

The topics to be discussed include everything related to the Bayesian approach to statistics: probabilistic modeling, causal inference, statistics of the empirical sciences (physics, atmosphere, biology, economics, ecology, etc.), decision making, pedagogy of statistics and probability theory, epistemology and methodology, data science, probabilistic programming languages (PyMC, Stan, Turing), Bayesian workflow, machine learning methods and artificial intelligence, etc.

We expect to achieve a succefull exchange of information, knowledge and experiences realted to Bayesian methods, promoting the development of Bayesian statistics. The meeting, by including tutorials, will not only allow the dissemination of the discipline, but will also generate a space for various actors to find an adequate and stimulating environment for discussion
